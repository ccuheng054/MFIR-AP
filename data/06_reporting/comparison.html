    Appendix A. Metrics and terms.

    General

    • Precision = TP/(TP+FP)
    • Recall = TP/(TP+FN)
    • Accuracy = (TP+TN)/(TP+TN+FP+FN)
    • ROC - receiver operating characteristic curve.
    • AUC - area under curve - is equal to the probability that a classifier will rank a randomly chosen positive instance higher than a randomly chosen negative one.

    Research-specific

    • APT - prediction time, always a positive number.
    • ATTA - prediction and detection time, any real number.

    Appendix B. Dataset and ablation variations.

    • Training set subjects: 9
      • 30 positive samples (each),
      • negative samples augmented by extracting patches in temporal dimension, positive:negative ratio balanced during training.
    • Testing set subjects: 3
      • 30 positive samples (each), around 1-1.5 min. long,
      • 30 negative smaples (each), around 1-1.5 min. long.

    Ablations

  • Frame - Frame and frame_shift ablation
  • View - View ablation
  • Downsampling - Input resolution study
  • Losses - Losses study
